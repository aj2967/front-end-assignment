Biometrics

Cars are going to be ever-closer monitoring drivers and occupants for many reasons, including making sure they're paying attention to the act of driving, optimizing safety systems in the event of a crash and to tailor infotainment experiences to individual passengers. Biometrics will be an important new way to help accomplish these goals.

Smart visors

Bosch's Virtual Visor is refreshingly easy to understand: It improves on the nearly century-old sun visor by offering up to 90% more visibility. How? By employing a transparent LCD visor that can be intelligently blacked-out section by section to only block those areas where the sun is impeding the driver's visibility. It does so with powerful software and a simple driver-facing camera.
